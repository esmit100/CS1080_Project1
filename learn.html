Common avalanche “red flags”

  • Recent avalanches nearby
  • Cracking shooting out from your feet/skis
  • “Whumphing” sounds (collapsing weak layer)
  • Heavy new snow or strong wind loading
  • Rapid warming or intense sun

If you see multiple red flags, choose simpler terrain or turn around.

Simple terrain reminders

  • Steeper slopes are generally more likely to slide.
  • Avoid terrain traps (gullies, creek beds, road cuts).
  • Spread out when crossing questionable areas.
